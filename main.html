 Interactive Project

Drion

Interactive Video Installation

Showcase:

2015 Simon Fraser University FCAT Undergraduate Conference

2015 Simon Fraser University Surrey Open House

Drion is an interactive installation art and video mapping. The concept revolves around the idea of balance and how to keep the body balance with visual illusion graphics. By creating an interactive installation themed under TRON, the participant experiences a fun way of balancing by holding a stick that would help them balance on a beam and follow the light on the ground. The harmonious background music will change along with the graphics depending on whether the participant is balanced on the beam or unbalanced.
